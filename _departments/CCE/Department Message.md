<h4><strong>Character and Citizenship Education (CCE)</strong></h4>
<p>The Character and Citizenship Education (CCE) Department is dedicated
to nurturing every child into a confident learner and caring individual—developing
them as <em>Thinkers Today, Leaders Tomorrow</em>.</p>
<p>Our programmes are thoughtfully designed to support the holistic development
of every student. We believe that every child can learn, serve, and shine
in their own unique way. Through our customised Values Education Programme,
students experience daily positive messaging and engaging classroom lessons
that strengthen important values such as respect, integrity, care, and
excellence. These are closely integrated with the Form Teacher Guidance
Period (FTGP), where students build strong social-emotional skills to navigate
relationships and challenges.</p>
<p>We adopt a whole-school approach to Positive Education, encouraging mindfulness
and a growth mindset so that students develop resilience, confidence, and
a love for learning. Our students are also guided to become active and
informed citizens, with opportunities to explore both national and global
issues through meaningful, age-appropriate discussions.</p>
<p>Beyond the classroom, our strong Case Management system ensures that every
child is well-supported in their emotional, learning, and developmental
needs. We work closely with teachers and parents to provide a caring and
enabling environment where each student can thrive.</p>
<p>Through these experiences, we aim to grow not only academically inclined
learners, but also empathetic individuals who are ready to contribute positively
to the community.</p>
