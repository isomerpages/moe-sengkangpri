<p>At Seng Kang Primary, our Mathematics Department is guided by the vision
to prepare SKLites into</p>
<p></p>
<blockquote>
<p>Confident, future-ready learners who are curious, thoughtful, and inspired
to serve and make a positive impact on the world.</p>
</blockquote>
<p>In line with Singapore’s Mathematics Curriculum Framework, mathematical
problem solving sits at the heart of our classrooms. We design learning
experiences focused on</p>
<p></p>
<blockquote>
<p>Developing future-ready students who can think critically, ask questions,
and make informed decisions.</p>
</blockquote>
<p>To engage our digital-native learners, we purposefully integrate EdTech
to make mathematical concepts interactive, collaborative, and accessible.</p>
<p>Recognising that every learner is unique, Mathematics is taught through
engaging, concrete experiences that build deep skills in inquiry, reasoning,
and application. By exploring authentic, real-world problems alongside
digital tools, students see firsthand how Mathematics connects to everyday
life.</p>
<p>Beyond academics, we emphasise core values, ethics, and positive attitudes.</p>
<p></p>
<blockquote>
<p>Students are guided to see the impact of mathematical logic on society
and the environment, helping them form thoughtful perspectives.</p>
</blockquote>
