<h4><strong>Learning Support Mathematics (LSM)</strong></h4>
<p></p>
<blockquote>
<p><strong>Early Intervention</strong>
</p>
</blockquote>
<p>Provides timely, targeted assistance for students struggling with basic
numeracy.</p>
<p></p>
<blockquote>
<p><strong>Skill Development</strong>
</p>
</blockquote>
<p>Focuses on bridging learning gaps and strengthening foundational mathematical
skills.</p>
<p></p>
<blockquote>
<p><strong>Progressive Support</strong>
</p>
</blockquote>
<p>Ensures students remain on track with the standard curriculum alongside
their peers.</p>
<h4><strong>Mathematics Special Programme (E2K Mathematics)</strong></h4>
<p>The <strong>E2K Mathematics Programme</strong> is an enrichment initiative
tailored for <strong>selected upper primary students</strong> (Primary 4
to 6) who demonstrate a keen interest and a strong aptitude and passion
for mathematics.</p>
<p>Through a specialized <strong>inquiry-based approach</strong>, the program
challenges these students to sharpen their mathematical reasoning and gain
a deeper, more sophisticated understanding of core concepts.</p>
<p></p>
<blockquote>
<p><strong>Inquiry-Based Learning</strong>
</p>
</blockquote>
<p>Focuses on deepening conceptual understanding and reasoning skills through
puzzles, games, and active exploration.</p>
<p></p>
<blockquote>
<p><strong>Collaborative Problem Solving</strong>
</p>
</blockquote>
<p>Encourages students to think like mathematicians, working both independently
and together to solve complex challenges.</p>
<p></p>
<blockquote>
<p><strong>Future-Ready Skills</strong>
</p>
</blockquote>
<p>Builds essential 21st-century competencies, including critical thinking,
innovative problem-solving, and effective communication.</p>
