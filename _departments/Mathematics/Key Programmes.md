<h4><strong>iSTRETCH Programme</strong></h4>
<p>The iSTRETCH (E2K) Mathematics programme stretches students beyond the
core curriculum by fostering critical thinking and effective communication.</p>
<p></p>
<blockquote>
<p>Through collaborative problem-solving, students learn to challenge assumptions,
approach complex challenges from multiple angles, and work together to
uncover solutions.</p>
</blockquote>
<p>Additionally, the programme emphasizes communication, requiring students
to clearly articulate, clarify, and defend their mathematical reasoning
to both their peers and teachers.</p>
<h4><strong>Math Infused Activities (National Day)</strong></h4>
<p>Celebrate National Day with math in motion!</p>
<p></p>
<blockquote>
<p>Our National Education (NE) activities transform patriotic celebrations
into a vibrant learning experience.</p>
</blockquote>
<p>Students use trundle wheels and measuring tapes to measure flags and distance
of the floor, size up National Day flags, and use protractors to master
angle measurements. By blending citizenship with critical thinking, we
make mathematical problem-solving meaningful, interactive, and deeply engaging.</p>

<h4><strong>Math Recess Play P5</strong></h4>
<p>Transforming abstract Primary 5 concepts into joyful discovery, our innovative
program brings mathematics to life.</p>
<p></p>
<blockquote>
<p>By using everyday canteen items, playground geometry, and active games,
students learn Mathematics concepts like fractions, ratios, and averages
through hands-on play.</p>
</blockquote>
<p>We build resilient, confident thinkers by turning unstructured playtime
into a robust, anxiety-free learning laboratory. Discover math in motion!</p>

<h4><strong>Math Learning Day</strong></h4>
<p>Math Learning Day transforms our school into a vibrant discovery hub where
mathematics comes alive for all levels!</p>
<p></p>
<blockquote>
<p>Ditching textbooks for hands-on exploration, students engage with real-world
objects and physical activities to uncover core mathematical concepts.</p>
</blockquote>
<p>From lower primary students counting with tactile counters to upper primary
students building 3D geometric models and measuring spaces, every activity
emphasizes experiential learning. By touching, moving, and collaborating,
students discover that math is a dynamic, fun, and natural part of life!</p>
<h4><strong>Math Competitions</strong></h4>
<p>Our P3 to P6 Math Olympiad competitions nurture students with an interest
in mathematics by introducing advanced, non-routine problem-solving.</p>
<p></p>
<blockquote>
<p>Beyond the standard curriculum, these competitions develop critical heuristics,
ignite logical reasoning, and build academic resilience.</p>
</blockquote>
<p>By challenging students early, we identify mathematical talent and provide
an engaging platform that builds deep confidence.</p>
