<p>At Seng Kang Primary School, our Science Department is guided by the belief
that “Thinkers Today, Leaders Tomorrow.” We nurture future-ready learners
who are curious, thoughtful, and able to make a positive impact on the
world.</p>
<p>Grounded in Singapore’s Science Curriculum Framework, we place Science
for Life and Society at the heart of learning. We aim to develop scientifically
literate students who can think critically, ask questions, and make informed
decisions. At the same time, we build strong foundations to support future
learning in STEM.</p>
<p>We recognise that every learner is different. Through engaging experiences,
students develop skills in inquiry, thinking, and application. Science
learning goes beyond facts. Students explore real-world issues and see
how Science connects to everyday life.</p>
<p>We also emphasise values, ethics, and attitudes. Students are guided to
consider the impact of Science on society and the environment, and to form
thoughtful perspectives.</p>
<p>Together with our teachers and partners, we create a supportive environment
where students grow as confident inquirers with a lasting interest in Science.</p>
