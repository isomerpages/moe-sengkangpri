<p>The VIA (Values in Action) programme aims to deepen students’ understanding
and practice of service and care. Through school-wide VIA initiatives,
students are provided with meaningful and authentic experiences that bring
values to life, strengthening their awareness and connection to the community.</p>
<p>Level-based VIA programmes are intentionally designed with clear learning
outcomes, enabling students to apply values purposefully in real-life contexts.
These experiences nurture empathy, responsibility, and social awareness,
shaping students into active and thoughtful contributors to society.&nbsp;
The projects finally apex at Primary 6 where pupils put together the entrepreneurship
and service skills learnt to a self-initiated project.</p>
<p>Selected students further extend their VIA journey through CCA partnerships
and collaborations with external organisations. These platforms provide
opportunities for sustained service, advocacy, and leadership, empowering
students to make a meaningful impact beyond the classroom.</p>
