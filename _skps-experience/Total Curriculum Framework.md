<h4><strong>Enhanced Total Curriculum Framework (eTCF)</strong></h4>
<p></p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/etcf.jpg">
</div>
<p>The Enhanced Total Curriculum Framework (eTCF) guides how we nurture Thinkers
Today and Leaders Tomorrow. It brings together our curriculum, character
development, and school culture under one clear direction.&nbsp;</p>
<p>Our eTCF ensures that teaching is purposeful and learning is engaging.
It aligns with our three strategic thrusts: ‘Students, Our Focus’ , ‘Staff,
Our Key’&nbsp; and ‘Partners, Our Leverage’. This alignment keeps our work
coherent and centred on the holistic growth of every student.</p>
<p>We place strong emphasis on the joy of teaching and learning. Teachers
design meaningful lessons using differentiated instruction, AI in Education,
and strategies from the science of learning. They also use balanced assessment
to support progress. Teaching is guided by clear intent, and assessment
is used to strengthen learning so that lessons remain rigorous, relevant,
and motivating.</p>
<p>The framework also develops key competencies for the future. Students
build communication, collaboration, and information skills. They learn
to think critically, adapt to change, and generate new ideas. They also
develop civic, global, and cross-cultural awareness so they can contribute
responsibly in a complex world.</p>
<p>Character development remains central to our work. Through Social-Emotional
Learning, National Education, and Values Education, we nurture resilience,
empathy, and integrity.</p>
<p>Guided by Positive Education and the PERMAH (Positive emotions, Engagement,
Relationships, Meaning, Accomplishments and Health) approach, we also create
a caring environment where students feel safe, valued, and empowered to
take ownership of their growth. Through the eTCF, we translate our vision
into daily practice and support every Seng Kang learner to grow into a
confident thinker and purposeful leader.</p>
